## Logo Variants

| Variant | Use on | Formats |
|---|---|---|
| **BLACK-GOLD** | White or light backgrounds | `.eps` `.jpg` `.png` `.svg` |
| **WHITE-GOLD** | Dark / black backgrounds | `.eps` `.png` `.svg` |

The chip symbol is also available standalone (BLACK, GOLD, WHITE) under `PayFacto-Logo/PayFacto - Chip/`.

### Logo Rules

- Always use the chip + wordmark together — never the wordmark alone
- Maintain a minimum clear space of **0.25"** on all sides
- Minimum size: **0.80" × 0.15"** (77 × 14.5 px)
- Do not distort, recolor, add effects, or remove the chip
